# Community Features

## Tipping

**Purpose**: Send USDC tips directly to tile owners to show appreciation for their content.

**How it works**:
- 100% of tip goes to tile owner
- No platform fee (0% taken by NonceArt)
- Minimum tip: 0.01 USDC

### Sending Tips

**How to tip**:
1. Click any tile on canvas
2. Pinned tooltip appears
3. Click "Tip" button (⭐️ star icon)
4. Tip dialog opens

**Tip dialog**:
- **Recipient**: Tile owner address (shown, not editable)
- **Amount**: Enter tip amount in USDC (minimum 0.01)
- **Terms of Service**: Must accept ToS
- **Preview**: Shows tile info and recipient

**Amount input**:
- Enter any amount ≥ 0.01 USDC
- No maximum (tip as much as you want)
- Defaults to 1.00 USDC

**Confirmation**:
1. Enter tip amount
2. Review recipient address
3. Accept Terms of Service
4. Click "Send Tip" button
5. Sign transaction
6. Wait for confirmation

**After tipping**:
- Tip appears in Activity feed as `TIP` (yellow label)
- Owner receives full amount
- Transaction recorded on blockchain

**Can tip**:
- ✅ Your own tiles (if desired)
- ✅ Any painted tile
- ✅ Unpainted tiles (owner still receives)
- ✅ Listed tiles (doesn't affect listing)

### Use Cases

- Appreciate great artwork
- Support talented artists
- Thank tile owners for entertaining content
- Compensate for inspired designs
- General goodwill

### Payment Requirements

Tip payment goes directly to owner (not treasury).

**Payment structure**:
- Recipient: Tile owner address
- Amount: Full tip amount (100%)
- No splits, no fees
